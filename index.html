Our Story

Refined cuisine, rooted in tradition.

Born from a love of seasonal ingredients and timeless technique, Maison Noir blends European elegance with contemporary boldness. Each plate is composed to delight the eye and the palate.

Led by Chef Antoine Laurent, our kitchen sources from local farms and the day's best market to bring you food that feels both familiar and unforgettable.
